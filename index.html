Crystal Collector

You will be presented with a computer generated random number at the beginning of the game. There are 4 crystals, each one will add a specific number to the player's score you win the game by matching the computer's score. You lse the game by going over the computer's score. The value of each crystal is hidden from the player until you click on it. The value of each crystal changes at the beginning of each game.
